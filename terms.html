Cancellation and Changes

We understand plans change. Our cancellation policy is as follows:

To cancel or modify a booking, contact us as early as possible by phone at (978) 255-3140 or email at info@seacoastrvip.com.

Airport Pickups and Wait Time

For airport pickups, we track your flight using the flight number provided. If your flight is delayed, your driver will adjust accordingly at no additional charge for the first 45 minutes of wait time beyond the original scheduled pickup time.

For non-airport pickups, your driver will wait up to 10 minutes at the scheduled pickup time. After 10 minutes without contact from the passenger, the booking may be treated as a no-show.
