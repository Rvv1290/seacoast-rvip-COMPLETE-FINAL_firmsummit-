Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Seacoast RVIP LLC ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect and use your information.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when booking a ride: your name, phone number, email address, pickup and destination addresses, and payment method preference.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to confirm and complete your ride reservation. We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal information to third parties.

Payment Information

Payment processing is handled securely through Square and Venmo. We do not store your credit card or payment details on our servers.

Communications

We may contact you via phone, text, or email to confirm your reservation, send payment links, or provide ride day reminders. You may opt out at any time by contacting us directly.
