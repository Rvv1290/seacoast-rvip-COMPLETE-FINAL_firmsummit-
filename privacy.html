Privacy Policy
Effective Date: January 1, 2025  ·  Last Updated: March 2025
Plain language summary: Seacoast RVIP collects only the information needed to book and complete your ride. We do not sell your data, we do not share it with advertisers, and we only contact you about your booking unless you ask otherwise.
Section 01
Who We Are

Seacoast RVIP LLC ("Seacoast RVIP," "we," "us," or "our") is a private pre-booked car service operating in the NH Seacoast area. We operate the website www.seacoastrvip.com to accept ride reservations, quote requests, and general inquiries.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at info@seacoastrvip.com or (978) 255-3140.

Section 02
Information We Collect

We collect information only when you voluntarily provide it through our booking, quote, or contact forms. This includes:

  • Identity: Your full name
  • Contact: Phone number and email address
  • Trip details: Pickup address, destination, date, time, passengers, luggage, and special requests
  • Payment preference: Your chosen method (card, Venmo, or cash). We do not store credit card numbers on our website.
  • Flight information: Flight number, when provided, to track delays
  • Account information: If you create a client account, we store your phone number as your identifier and your trip history

We also collect standard technical data automatically (browser type, device, pages visited, approximate location) through our hosting provider, Vercel.

Section 03
How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to operate our transportation service:

  • To confirm, schedule, and complete your ride
  • To send booking confirmations, ride reminders, and payment links
  • To respond to quote requests and inquiries
  • To generate receipts and maintain your ride history
  • To contact you about changes to your booking or day-of coordination
  • To improve our website and service quality

We do not use your information for automated profiling, targeted advertising, or any purpose unrelated to your transportation.

Section 04
How We Store and Protect Your Information

Your booking data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, a secure cloud database with encryption at rest and in transit. Access is restricted to authorized Seacoast RVIP personnel only.

Form submissions are processed through FormSubmit and delivered to our business email. Payment processing is handled by Square and Venmo, which have their own security standards and privacy policies.

We retain your booking information for up to 3 years for accounting and service history. To request earlier deletion, email info@seacoastrvip.com.

Section 05
Sharing of Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share it only in these limited cases:

  • With your driver: Name, pickup, destination, and trip details shared to complete your service
  • With payment processors: Amount due and booking reference shared with Square or Venmo when you pay
  • As required by law: When required by valid legal process or government authority
Section 06
Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies — primarily to keep you logged into your client account. We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking.

Google Maps and Google Fonts may set their own cookies per Google's privacy policy. You can disable cookies in your browser, but this may affect your client account functionality.

Section 07
Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, and to opt out of non-essential communications. Contact us at info@seacoastrvip.com and we will respond within 10 business days.

Section 08
Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we have, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

Section 09
Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The "Last Updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions About Your Privacy?

We take your privacy seriously. Reach out directly with any questions or requests.
