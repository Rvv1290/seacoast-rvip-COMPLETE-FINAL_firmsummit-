Last updated: April 2, 2025

1. Acceptance of Terms

By submitting a booking request you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service.

2. Reservations

A reservation is not confirmed until you receive written confirmation by email or text. Change requests must be made at least 24 hours before pickup.

3. Cancellation & Refund Policy

✅ 24+ hours before pickup — Full refund of any deposit paid.

⚠️ 12–24 hours before pickup — 50% cancellation fee applies.

❌ Less than 12 hours — 100% of fare due. No refund.

❌ No-show — 100% of fare due after 15 min wait.

No smoking, vaping, open containers, or illegal substances. Passenger count may not exceed vehicle capacity. Damage to the vehicle will be billed to the account.
