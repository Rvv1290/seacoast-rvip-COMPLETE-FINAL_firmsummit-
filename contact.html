Essentials & Policies

Reservations can be made through our digital portal or via our concierge desk. We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance to guarantee your preferred vehicle selection.
We utilize real-time flight telemetry. Your chauffeur will automatically adjust their arrival time based on actual landing schedules, ensuring a seamless exit from the terminal.
Cancellations made more than 12 hours prior to the scheduled pick-up are eligible for a full refund. Late cancellations may incur a service fee.
We accept all major credit cards, corporate account billing, and secure digital payments. We accept Cash, Venmo, Cash App, Apple Pay, and all major debit and credit cards.
Our fleet includes SUVs (6 passengers), Black SUVs (up to 7 passengers). All vehicles are late-model and impeccably maintained.
While we primarily serve the Seacoast and major metropolitan hubs, we offer long-distance interstate transfers upon request.
